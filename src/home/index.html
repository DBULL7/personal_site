Hi, I'm Devon.

I'm a data minded, performance oriented Software Engineer. I believe performance is a product and I strive to build apps that perform and deliver quality user experiences on all platforms.

I'm a recent graduate from the Frontend program of the Turing School of Software and Design in Denver, CO. Turing is a 7 month program that taught me how to build everything from Single Page Applications to Progressive Web Applications along with native apps. Before Turing I attended the University of Kansas with a degree in Economics and a minor in business. Upon graduating I joined a startup with friends. I tried coding to help the dev team and fell in love with programming. Instead of going back to school for 2 and a half years to learn theory I chose Turing to get actual development experience at a breakneck pace.

Due to my background with Economics and startups I like to deal with large datasets and try to optimize my apps to save on development costs and server costs to the business. I believe in Agile Development.

Upon graduating from Turing I've moved to Silicon Valley to pursue my dream of helping to change the world. When I'm not coding I like to golf, read up on new development tools/techniques or startups, and visit friends.
